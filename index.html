Introduction

My name is Igor Zboran. I am an independent scientist and software developer with over twenty-five years of experience in research and development in the Information and Communications Technology (ICT) industry. I want to transform my knowledge into a practical system or service that people would love to use.


Over the past few years, I have noticed a decline in email infrastructure. Email delivery is only reliable among a few global providers. Large attachments are shared via their silos. Spam is a constant issue. To address these issues, I have written two white papers: Cargomail, a revised email system, and the Global Reference Identity Protocol (GRIP). With this fresh outlook on the email system, we can envision a world where email is a means rather than an obstacle to communication. The world where you can exchange messages, documents, images, videos, and audio without restrictions. Please take the time to review, evaluate, and provide feedback on my effort to create a better world.
