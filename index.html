Identity-Based Privacy

I am a software engineer with over twenty years accumulated experience in the software architecture, development and product design. I'd like to transform my knowledge to a useful product or service that customers wish to use and buy.

I have designed a new decentralized Identity-Based Privacy (IBP) trusted model based on OpenID Connect and User Managed Access standards. IBP model supports Privacy by Design principle (PbD). Since November 2013, I have been working on practical B2B/B2C data sharing and data exchange application based on IBP model that is a better fit for the distributed nature of cloud computing.
