Rich Communication Suite

Working economically means connecting people and devices inside and outside the enterprise in a way that provides real-time communications and real-time access to information in the context of the activity.

When businesses are looking for a decentralized file-sharing application as the basis of a collaboration initiative, the most common solutions are built on FTP platform. But FTP servers are difficult to use for partners behind corporate firewall. Security and limited ability to control permissions is another major issue with FTP servers. There is no easy way to see who accessed what.

Skype is not a very acceptable platform for business communications. Lack of privacy and missing Identity and Access Management integration make Skype incompatible with enterprise security policies.

The next stage in the evolution of the internet is a network of interconnected objects. The Internet of Things (IoT) involves interaction, communication and exchange of data between people, vehicles, machines, devices and sensors. A new advanced applications and services that need to communicate with IoT, require fast reliable and manageable connections.

WebRTC - a technology developed by Google, now being drafted as an API definition by the W3C, enables for real-time, peer-to-peer audio, video, and data communication between browsers. In combination with a modern Identity Relationship Management (IRM) allows for ad hoc communication beyond traditional enterprise boundaries, while staying compliant with security policies.

Rich Communication Suite (RCS) focuses on person-to-person and person-to-machine communication and data exchange to establish easier and more profitable collaboration. As an email, Skype, Dropbox and FTP alternative, RCS helps users collaborate and manage critical content simply and securely.

An advantage of using RCS is that you gain access to all of your colleagues, partners and customers on the enterprise network easily and this allows you quickly start communicating with both internal and external participants. RCS integrates communication between people and IoT into one easy manageable system.

Peer-to-peer distributed application architecture protects enterprise intellectual property and provides flexible security.

Opportunities:

  • Internet of Everything
  • Managed File Transfer (MFT) alternative
  • Product Design and Development (engineering) collaboration
  • Science & Research teams collaboration
  • Supply chain collaboration
  • Healthcare communication services
