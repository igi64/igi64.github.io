The Cross-Domain Cloud Storage

Working economically means connecting people inside and outside the enterprise in a way that provides real time communications and real time access to information in the context of the activity.

When businesses are looking for a file-sharing application as the basis of a collaboration initiative, the most common solutions are built on FTP platform. But FTP servers are difficult to use for partners behind corporate firewall. Security and limited ability to control permissions is another major issue with FTP servers. There is no easy way to see who accessed what.

Modern data storage must support ad hoc work groups that need to reach beyond traditional enterprise boundaries.

AiryDrive - The Cross-Domain Cloud Storage focuses on B2B/B2C data sharing and exchange to establish easier and more profitable collaboration. As an email, FTP and Dropbox alternative, AiryDrive helps users collaborate and manage critical content simply and securely.

The Cross-Domain Cloud Storage allows teams to quickly share important information, send large files, and collaborate with both internal and external partners. On-premises data storage protects enterprise intellectual property and provides flexible security.

Opportunities:

  • Product Design and Development (engineering) data storage
  • Data storage for Science & Research teams
  • Supply chain collaboration
  • Product information/Supply chain/BOM transparency
  • Healthcare enterprise data storage
  • Data Exchange Hub
