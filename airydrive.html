The Multi-Party Cloud Storage

All businesses benefit from being more connected to their customers, suppliers and partners. AiryDrive - the Multi-Party Cloud Storage focuses on B2B and B2C data sharing and exchange to establish easier and more profitable collaboration.

As an FTP Alternative, Document Management or Engineering Data Storage, AiryDrive helps users collaborate and manage critical content simply and securely.

Modern data storage must support ad hoc work groups that need to reach beyond traditional enterprise boundaries and at times include customers, partners and suppliers.

FTP server is difficult to use for partners and administrators are worried about its security and limited ability to control permissions.

AiryDrive allows teams to quickly share important information, send large files, and collaborate with both internal and external partners. On-premises data storage protects enterprise intellectual property and provides flexible security.

AiryDrive is an Open Source Software project hosted on GitHub.
